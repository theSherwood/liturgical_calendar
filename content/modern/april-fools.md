## Meaning

A day of licensed mischief with roots reaching back to Roman Hilaria and medieval feasts of fools. Every healthy culture keeps a day when the rules relax and everyone's allowed to be a little ridiculous. Laughter is load-bearing.

## Observance

Harmless pranks only — the good kind that end in laughter, never tears. Set the bar at 'clever,' not 'cruel.' Best prank of the day gets bragging rights until next April.
