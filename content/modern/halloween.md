## Meaning

The living, laughing descendant of ancient Samhain — the night we dress up as what scares us and go door to door meeting our neighbors. It is one of the last great communal street rituals we have left, and it runs on nothing but candy and good will.

## Observance

Costumes for everyone, adults included — no exceptions. Carve pumpkins, walk the neighborhood, meet the neighbors. Watch something gently spooky and eat too much candy.
