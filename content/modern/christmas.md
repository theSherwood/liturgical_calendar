## Meaning

Whatever else it has meant, Christmas is the midwinter festival humanity keeps returning to — a day of family, generosity, and light kindled against the dark. You don't have to believe the theology to keep the warmth; the warmth was always the point.

## Observance

Gifts in the morning, a big meal, and no agenda but each other. The rule of the day: be generous, be present, and don't check the time. Call the people you can't be with.
