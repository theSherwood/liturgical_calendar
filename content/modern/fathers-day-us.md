## Meaning

The counterpart to Mother's Day, and just as needed. A day for the fathers and everyone who has fathered anyone — for showing up, steadying, and providing, often without being thanked for it.

## Observance

The fathers pick the day's activity and are excused from all chores. A handwritten note beats any gadget; say the specific thing you're grateful for.
