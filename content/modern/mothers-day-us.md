## Meaning

A day set aside for the least-thanked and most essential work there is. Honoring the mothers among us — and everyone who has mothered anyone — is the kind of gratitude that should be constant but at least gets one guaranteed day.

## Observance

The mothers do nothing today; everyone else does everything. Breakfast made for them, a handwritten note, and the actual words said out loud.

## Reading

> "A mother is she who can take the place of all others but whose place no one else can take." — Cardinal Mermillod
