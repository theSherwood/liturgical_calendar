## Meaning

A day of remembrance for those who died in the country's service, and — more quietly — the unofficial doorway into summer. The two moods aren't in conflict: gratitude for a life freely lived is exactly the right way to honor a life given.

## Observance

A moment of quiet at some point in the day for those who didn't come home. Then, in their honor, the first proper cookout of the summer — because the point of the sacrifice was days exactly like this one.

## Reading

> "The willingness of America's veterans to sacrifice for our country has earned them our lasting gratitude." — Jeff Miller
