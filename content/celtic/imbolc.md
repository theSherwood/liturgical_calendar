## Meaning

Halfway between the winter solstice and the spring equinox, when the ewes begin to milk and the very first green shows. Nothing has bloomed yet, but the year has quietly decided to live. Imbolc is hope before there is evidence for it.

## Observance

A hearth day. Light candles at dinner, clean out one small corner of the house to make room for the year, and name one thing you're tending quietly that hasn't shown results yet.
