## Meaning

The first of the three harvest festivals, when the first grain is cut and the year's long work starts paying out. It's a day about effort rewarded — the loaf on the table came from months of sun and rain and labor.

## Observance

Bake bread together, or buy a really good loaf and break it by hand. As you eat, each person names one thing they worked hard at this year that's finally bearing fruit.
