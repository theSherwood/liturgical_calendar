## Meaning

Whatever the theology, Easter is the spring festival that Europe has kept under one name or another since long before it had that name — a celebration of new life bursting out of a world that looked dead all winter. Eggs and hares and green shoots are older than any church: they are what spring looks like. It is the year's great festival of renewal and the second chance.

## Observance

Dye and hunt for eggs; the children lead. Fill the house with flowers and open every window. Whatever felt buried or finished over the winter, name it, and ask what might yet come back to life. A big bright meal to welcome the spring.
