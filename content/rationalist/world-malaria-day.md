## Meaning

Malaria still kills over half a million people a year, most of them children under five, and almost all of it is preventable with a bed net that costs a couple of dollars. It is the plainest example of the effective-altruist insight: not all good is equal, and some of the most powerful good you can do in the world is quiet, cheap, unglamorous, and far away. A life saved in a village you'll never see counts exactly as much as one saved next door.

## Observance

Give to a bed-net charity together, and look up how many nets your gift buys and roughly what that does. Talk about the difference between giving that feels good and giving that does good — and why we should want the second even when the first is more tempting.
