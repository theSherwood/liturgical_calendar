## Meaning

The birthday of a physicist who was equally a magician and a clown — who cracked safes, played bongos, drew the diagrams that reshaped physics, and above all refused to pretend to understand things he didn't. Feynman is the patron saint of honest curiosity.

## Observance

Ask 'why?' about something ordinary until you hit the bottom of what you actually know — then look it up together. Take real joy in finding something out. Don't fool yourself.

## Reading

> "The first principle is that you must not fool yourself — and you are the easiest person to fool."
> — Richard Feynman

He cracked safes, played bongos, drew the diagrams that remade physics, and refused all his life to pretend to understand what he didn't. Feynman is the patron saint of honest curiosity: the joy of finding things out, and the discipline of not kidding yourself about what you've found.
