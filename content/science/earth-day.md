## Meaning

A day for the only home our species has, and the only one it's likely to have for a long while. Every person you've ever loved lives on this single pale blue dot. Earth Day is the reminder to act like we know that.

## Observance

Do one concrete thing for the planet: pick up litter on a walk, plant something, fix rather than replace, or just spend the day outside appreciating what's being cared for.

## Reading

> "Look again at that dot. That's here. That's home. That's us. On it everyone you love, everyone you know, everyone you ever heard of, every human being who ever was, lived out their lives."
> — Carl Sagan, Pale Blue Dot

There is, so far, exactly one place in the universe known to hold life, and we are standing on it. Every person you will ever love shares this single fragile rock. Earth Day is just the reminder to act like we know that.
