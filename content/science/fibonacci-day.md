## Meaning

The date reads 1, 1, 2, 3 — the opening of the Fibonacci sequence, where each number is the sum of the two before it. It's the secret pattern behind pinecones, sunflower seeds, nautilus shells, and the spiral of galaxies. Nature, it turns out, can count.

## Observance

Hunt for the pattern in the real world — count the spirals on a pinecone or a sunflower, look for the golden spiral. Start the sequence out loud and see how far the family can go.
