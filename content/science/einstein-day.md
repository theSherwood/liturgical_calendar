## Meaning

A patent clerk who couldn't get an academic job sat and thought harder about light, space, and time than anyone before him — and discovered that time slows, space bends, and mass and energy are the same thing. Einstein rebuilt our picture of reality from the ground up, twice, mostly by imagining. That his birthday lands on Pi Day is a happy accident the universe seems to have arranged on purpose: the poet of space-time, sharing his day with the number hidden in every circle.

## Observance

Share the day with Pi Day and let them amplify each other — pie for the number, a thought experiment for the man. Ride an imaginary beam of light, or drop two different things and watch them land together. Take seriously that the deepest discoveries can start with a daydream.
