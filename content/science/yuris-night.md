## Meaning

The anniversary of the morning a human being first left the Earth — Yuri Gagarin, 1961, one orbit, 108 minutes, and our species was never quite planet-bound again. For all of history the sky was a ceiling; on this day it became a door.

## Observance

A 'world space party,' as it's actually called. Watch launch footage, look up at the sky after dark, and talk about the fact that there are people living up there right now, going around and around.

## Reading

> "Circling the Earth in my orbital spaceship I marveled at the beauty of our planet. People, let us preserve and increase this beauty, not destroy it."
> — Yuri Gagarin

For all of history the sky was a ceiling. On 12 April 1961, for 108 minutes, it became a door — and one man saw the whole Earth at once, small and blue and unbordered, the way we all secretly know it is.
