## Meaning

The friendliest math holiday there is: 3/14, for π, the number hiding in every circle, wave, and orbit in the universe. It never ends and never repeats, and it also happens to be Einstein's birthday. Mostly, though, it is a socially sanctioned excuse to eat pie.

## Observance

Eat pie — savory and sweet both count. Memorize a few digits of π together (3.14159…), or measure a round thing and a string and discover π for yourselves.

## Reading
