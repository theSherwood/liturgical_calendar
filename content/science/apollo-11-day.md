## Meaning

The single most audacious thing our species has ever done: on this day in 1969, two people stood on the Moon and a third circled overhead, while a fifth of all humanity watched from home. It was made possible by hundreds of thousands of people, slide rules, and nerve. For one day the whole planet looked up at the same rock and felt the same thing.

## Observance

Look at the Moon tonight and really see it — that is a place people have stood. Watch the landing footage. Talk about what it takes, in people and patience, to do something that hard.
