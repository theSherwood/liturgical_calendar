## Meaning

The birthday of the scientist who, more than anyone, taught ordinary people to feel the scale and wonder of the cosmos — and to demand good evidence while they did it. Sagan married awe and skepticism so gracefully that neither had to lose. We are, as he said, a way for the universe to know itself.

## Observance

Watch a bit of Cosmos, or read the 'Pale Blue Dot' passage aloud under the night sky. Hold two things at once, the way Sagan did: boundless wonder, and a refusal to believe things without good reason.
