## Meaning

The birthday of a US Navy admiral and computing pioneer who insisted computers could be taught to understand something closer to human language — an idea most experts called impossible. She was right, and every programming language since owes her. She also, famously, taped a real moth into a logbook and gave us the word 'debugging.'

## Observance

Try a little code together — even one line, even a kids' block-coding game. Adopt her best motto for a problem you're stuck on. And when something breaks today, call it a 'bug' and go find the moth.
