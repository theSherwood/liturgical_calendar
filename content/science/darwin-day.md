## Meaning

The birthday of the man who saw that all living things — every beetle, whale, and human — are literal cousins on one enormous family tree, shaped over unimaginable time by a single, simple, ruthless, beautiful idea. Few thoughts have ever rearranged the world so completely.

## Observance

Go look at some life — a walk outdoors, a nature documentary, a visit to see animals. Trace one creature's kinship to another. Marvel that it all came from one beginning.

## Reading

> "There is grandeur in this view of life… from so simple a beginning endless forms most beautiful." — Charles Darwin
