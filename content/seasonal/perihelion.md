## Meaning

Today Earth is as close to the sun as it ever gets — and yet it's the dead of northern winter. A small, useful lesson lives here: the seasons come from the planet's tilt, not its distance. It's a good day to be reminded how easily our intuitions about the world are wrong.

## Observance

A quiet science moment. Look up how far away 'closest' actually is (about 147 million km — still enormous), and talk about one thing this year that turned out to be the opposite of how it felt.
