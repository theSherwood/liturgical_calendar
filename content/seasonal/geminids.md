## Meaning

The Geminids are the year's most generous meteor shower, but they make you earn it in the December cold. The reward for bundling up is a dark, clear sky throwing more stars than any night in summer.

## Observance

Blankets, hot chocolate, hats, and the darkest yard you can find. Bundle up and go out for even fifteen minutes. A single bright fireball is worth the cold.
