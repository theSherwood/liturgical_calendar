## Meaning

Every August, Earth plows through the dust trail of an ancient comet, and for a few warm nights the sky throws sparks. The Perseids are the friendliest meteor shower of the year — warm enough to lie out under, generous enough to reward patience.

## Observance

Stay up late, drive somewhere dark, and lie on your backs. No phones — eyes need twenty minutes to adjust. Everyone counts their own shooting stars and calls them out. First to a dozen wins nothing, which is the point.
