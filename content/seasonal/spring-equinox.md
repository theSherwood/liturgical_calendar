## Meaning

Day and night stand exactly equal, and after today the light wins. It is the oldest new year on Earth — Babylonians, Persians, and Romans all began their year about now, when the ground softens and things start again.

## Observance

Plant something — seeds indoors, a tree outdoors, whatever the season allows. Balance an egg on its end if you like the old trick. Open every window and let the house exhale the winter.
