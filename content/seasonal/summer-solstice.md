## Meaning

The longest day of the year, the sun at the very top of its arc. From here the days shorten again, so the light is worth spending while we have this much of it. This is the year at full flood.

## Observance

Be outside from before sunrise to after sunset if you can. Eat dinner outdoors. Watch the sun go down together and don't rush it — it's the latest it will set all year.
