## Meaning

One of the oldest Athenian festivals — three days when the new wine was opened, children were crowned with flowers, and the spirits of the dead were said to walk the city before being gently sent home again. Joy and mortality braided together, which is very Greek.

## Observance

Open something you've been saving. Give the youngest child their first (tiny, symbolic) taste of the grown-up world. Pour a small libation and name someone gone.
