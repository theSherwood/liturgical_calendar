title: "Harvest & Remembrance"
blurb: "Gathering in — gratitude, the dead, and getting ready."
---

Autumn is the great turning inward. The harvest comes in, the light and the leaves fall away, and the year leans toward the dark half. It is the season of gratitude and of memory — of counting what we gathered, honoring the people we've lost, and quietly getting ready for what's coming. To give thanks and to prepare are the same gesture here: both are ways of facing the winter unafraid.
