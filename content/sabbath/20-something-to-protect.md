## Meaning

This reading closes the loop on the whole block. Learning to change your mind, to doubt well, to leave a tribe — none of it holds unless you have something you care about more than being seen as rational. If clear thinking exists only to admire itself, it curdles into a hobby of clever people scoring points. But when there is something real at stake — a person, a project, a world you want to leave better — you suddenly cannot afford to fool yourself, because self-deception now has a cost you refuse to pay. The thing you love is what keeps your honesty honest.

## Observance

Ask the question the essay asks: what do we care about enough that we would rather be corrected than comforted? Name one thing, as a family, for which you would want the truth even if it hurt. Let that be the reason the whole spring block was worth reading.

## Reading
